POSITION: DevOps / Platform Engineer
COMPANY: CloudEdge Infrastructure Ltd.
LOCATION: London, UK (Remote-first)

ABOUT THE ROLE
CloudEdge builds multi-tenant cloud infrastructure tooling for enterprise customers across Europe
and North America. We are looking for an experienced DevOps / Platform Engineer to own our
Kubernetes-based deployment platform, CI/CD pipelines, and cloud cost management strategy.

RESPONSIBILITIES
- Design, build, and maintain Kubernetes clusters (EKS / GKE) for production workloads
- Manage infrastructure-as-code using Terraform and Helm
- Build and maintain CI/CD pipelines (GitHub Actions, Jenkins, ArgoCD)
- Automate operational tasks with Python scripting
- Drive cloud cost optimisation across AWS and GCP
- Implement and maintain observability stack (Prometheus, Grafana, ELK / EFK)
- Enforce security best practices: RBAC, secrets management (Vault), network policies
- Mentor junior engineers and contribute to runbooks and documentation
- Participate in on-call rota for platform incidents

REQUIREMENTS
- 5+ years of DevOps / Platform / SRE engineering experience
- Expert-level Kubernetes knowledge (CKA or equivalent experience preferred)
- Hands-on experience with Terraform for cloud infrastructure provisioning
- Strong AWS skills; GCP or Azure experience is a bonus
- Proficiency in Python for automation and tooling
- Experience with container runtimes (Docker, containerd) and image registries
- Solid understanding of networking (VPC, DNS, load balancing, ingress controllers)
- Experience with at least one CI/CD platform (GitHub Actions, Jenkins, ArgoCD, Tekton)
- Familiarity with GitOps workflows

PREFERRED
- AWS Certified DevOps Engineer or AWS Solutions Architect certification
- Experience with service mesh technologies (Istio, Linkerd)
- Background in FinTech or SaaS platform engineering
- Experience with Ansible or Chef/Puppet for configuration management

COMPENSATION
- £75,000 – £95,000 per annum
- Remote-first with optional co-working spaces in London and Berlin
- Equipment budget and annual learning allowance
