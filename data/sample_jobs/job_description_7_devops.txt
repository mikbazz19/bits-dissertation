DevOps / Platform Engineer
CloudNative Corp | London, UK (Remote-first)

ROLE OVERVIEW
We are looking for a DevOps or Platform Engineer to join our infrastructure team. You will own
the reliability, scalability, and developer experience of our cloud-native platform.

- 4+ years in a DevOps, SRE, or Platform Engineering role
- Expert-level Kubernetes knowledge (workloads, RBAC, networking, storage)
- Terraform proficiency (modules, state management, remote backends)
- Strong scripting in Python or Bash
- Hands-on AWS experience (EKS, EC2, S3, IAM, VPC, RDS, CloudWatch)
- CI/CD pipeline design and implementation
- Container orchestration: Docker and Kubernetes
- Git-based workflows and GitOps principles

REQUIRED TECHNICAL SKILLS

- Programming/Scripting: Python, Bash
