POSITION: Senior Software Engineer – Backend
COMPANY: Meridian Financial Systems
LOCATION: New York, NY (Hybrid / Remote)

ABOUT THE ROLE
Meridian Financial Systems builds core banking and payments infrastructure for mid-market
financial institutions. We are hiring a Senior Backend Engineer to strengthen our services team.
We value pragmatic engineers with a track record of delivering reliable, maintainable software.

RESPONSIBILITIES
- Architect and implement high-throughput backend services in Python and/or Java
- Design data models and optimize queries for PostgreSQL and Cassandra
- Build and maintain event-driven pipelines using Kafka
- Participate in architecture reviews and contribute to technical roadmap
- Collaborate cross-functionally with product, QA, and DevOps teams
- Support production systems through on-call responsibilities

REQUIREMENTS
- 7+ years of professional software engineering experience
- Strong proficiency in Python and/or Java
- Experience designing and building distributed backend services
- Familiarity with relational databases (PostgreSQL preferred) and NoSQL stores
- Understanding of asynchronous messaging patterns (Kafka, RabbitMQ, or similar)
- Experience with REST and gRPC API design
- Comfortable working in Linux environments

PREFERRED
- Background in fintech, banking, or high-compliance regulated industries
- Experience with Kubernetes or containerised deployments
- Familiarity with Spring Boot (Java) or FastAPI / Django (Python)

NOTE ON EMPLOYMENT HISTORY
We understand that careers are not always linear. Career breaks for personal, family, or health
reasons are fully respected. We assess candidates on their skills and impact, not continuity.

COMPENSATION
- $145,000 – $175,000 base salary
- Annual performance bonus
- Comprehensive benefits package including 401(k) matching
