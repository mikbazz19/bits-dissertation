POSITION: Senior Backend Engineer
COMPANY: NexLayer Technologies
LOCATION: Chicago, IL (Hybrid)

ABOUT THE ROLE
NexLayer Technologies is looking for a Senior Backend Engineer to join our Platform team.
You will design, build, and maintain scalable web APIs and backend services that power our
SaaS platform used by over 500,000 end users.

RESPONSIBILITIES
- Design and develop RESTful APIs using Python (Django / FastAPI / Flask)
- Lead a small team of 2–4 engineers on feature delivery
- Collaborate with frontend teams on API contracts and integration
- Review code, mentor junior engineers, and drive best practices
- Improve system reliability, observability, and performance
- Participate in on-call rotation for production issues

REQUIREMENTS
- 6+ years of backend software development experience
- Strong proficiency in Python; experience with Django, FastAPI, or Flask
- Solid understanding of REST API design and HTTP fundamentals
- Experience with JavaScript / Node.js is a plus
- Experience with React or Vue for occasional full-stack work is a bonus
- Familiarity with relational databases (PostgreSQL, MySQL)
- Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
- Strong communication skills and ability to work independently
- Computer Science degree or equivalent practical experience

PREFERRED
- Experience leading or mentoring a small engineering team
- Exposure to microservices architecture
- Familiarity with Docker and container-based deployments

WHAT WE OFFER
- Competitive salary ($130,000 – $160,000)
- Health, dental, and vision insurance
- Remote-friendly culture with flexible hours
- Annual learning & development budget
