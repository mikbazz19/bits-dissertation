Data Analyst / Business Intelligence Developer
RetailMax Group | Remote (India)

ABOUT THE ROLE
We are looking for a talented individual who can bridge the gap between raw business data and
actionable insights. This hybrid role covers both advanced SQL analytics and dashboard development.

WHAT YOU WILL DO
- Query and analyse large datasets to support business decision-making
- Build and maintain dashboards in Tableau and Power BI
- Work with the data engineering team to understand data models and pipelines
- Translate business questions into SQL queries and present findings to stakeholders
- Identify data quality issues and propose remediation
- Conduct ad-hoc analysis for marketing, sales, and operations teams
- Collaborate with product teams to define and track KPIs

WHO YOU ARE
You have a strong SQL background and an eye for data quality. You are comfortable building charts
and dashboards. You communicate well in writing and verbally. You ask "why" before diving in.

WE EXPECT
- Advanced SQL (window functions, CTEs, complex joins, optimisation)
- Experience with at least one BI tool (Tableau preferred, Power BI acceptable)
- Python or R for data wrangling and statistical analysis
- Familiarity with a cloud data warehouse: Snowflake, BigQuery, or Redshift
- Experience presenting data findings to non-technical stakeholders
- Attention to detail and strong data quality instincts
- 2–5 years of relevant analytics or BI experience

NICE TO HAVE
- Experience with dbt (data build tool) for data transformation
- Exposure to Looker or similar modern BI platforms
- Basic knowledge of machine learning concepts (regression, clustering)
- Experience in retail, e-commerce, or consumer goods domain
- Excel expertise (PivotTables, VLOOKUP, advanced formulas)

EDUCATION
- Bachelor's degree in Statistics, Mathematics, Economics, Computer Science, or related field
- Master's degree appreciated but not required

COMPENSATION
- ₹8 – 15 LPA depending on experience
- Annual performance bonus up to 15%
- Fully remote with quarterly team meet-ups in Mumbai
- Learning budget ₹25,000 per year
