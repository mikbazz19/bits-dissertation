Junior Software Developer (0-1 Year Experience / Fresh Graduate)
TechStart Solutions | Bangalore, India (Hybrid)

ABOUT US
TechStart Solutions is a product startup building SaaS tools for SMEs. We believe in growing talent
from the ground up. This role is ideal for fresh graduates or candidates with up to 1 year of experience.

ROLE SUMMARY
We are looking for a motivated Junior Software Developer who is eager to learn, takes initiative,
and wants to grow fast in a startup environment. You will be closely mentored by senior engineers.

RESPONSIBILITIES
- Develop and maintain features for our web application under the guidance of senior developers
- Write clean, readable code with appropriate unit tests
- Participate in daily standups, sprint planning, and retrospectives
- Learn and contribute to code reviews
- Document your work and update technical wikis
- Help triage and fix bugs reported by users

MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS
- Bachelor's degree (or equivalent) in Computer Science, Information Technology, or related field
- Freshers welcome — 0 to 1 year of professional or internship experience
- Basic proficiency in at least ONE backend language: Python, Java, or Node.js
- Fundamental understanding of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
- Familiarity with SQL (simple SELECT queries, basic table design)
- Understanding of version control with Git (commit, push, pull request)
- Ability to read and understand existing code
- Growth mindset and willingness to ask questions

GOOD TO HAVE (NOT REQUIRED)
- Any academic or personal project on GitHub
- Basic exposure to REST APIs (consuming or building)
- Familiarity with any cloud platform (AWS free tier, GCP free tier)
- Any internship experience in software development
- Problem-solving track record (LeetCode, HackerRank, Codechef etc.)

TRAINING & ONBOARDING
All selected candidates go through a 30-day structured onboarding programme covering:
- Company tech stack deep-dive
- Code standards and practices
- Pair programming sessions with a buddy engineer

EDUCATION REQUIREMENTS
- BE / B.Tech / BSc / BCA in Computer Science, IT, or related — 2023 or 2024 batch preferred
- 60% or above in degree (or CGPA 6.0+)

COMPENSATION & BENEFITS
- CTC: ₹4.0 – ₹6.5 LPA (based on assessment)
- Stock options after probation
- Sponsored online learning (Udemy, Coursera)
- Flexible work-from-home Fridays
- Health insurance (self + family)

TechStart Solutions is an equal-opportunity employer.
